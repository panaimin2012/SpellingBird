About Spelling Bird

SpellingBird is a personal software for students who use Longman Elect series book to practise spelling

This software is made for my daughter who is a P3 student in the Chekiang & Kiangsu Primary School in HK. I will be constantly adding words for new units while she makes progress in her study.

License

SpellingBird is a free software. You can use it, modify it and distribute it at no cost.

How to play

Touch any place to start a game. Find characters of the word and touch them in order. Characters touched must be adjacent.

How to select book and unit

Click the book icon, and in the books screen, click book to show units, click unit to select. Current books:
  • Longman Elect 4A (2 units for now)
  • Longman Elect 3A
  • Longman Elect 3B
  • Longman Elect 2A
  • Longman Elect 2B
